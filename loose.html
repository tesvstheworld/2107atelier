Project Loose Media

To design a modern and functional workspace that promotes efficiency and foster productivity as individual workers and as a team.

Year: 2021 Location: Lagos Client: Loose Media

Project Brief

Loose Media, one of the fastest growing media marketing companies engaged our services for their commercial interior design project. The building located at Yaba was initially a 5 bedroom residential duplex. They required design consultancy and implementation services for their office, and they wanted to create a warm and motivating workplace environment for their employees to encourage productivity and collaboration.

For this project one of our clients concern was space, we maximized the space to fit in all the requirements of our client and we went with designing the ground floor of their office to promote productivity, teamwork and efficiency by its users, which housed a reception area that was designed for optimum comfort for visitors to relax, General office, conference room, Breakout room, lounge (lunch room) & a mini work pod under the stairs. While the first floor which contains a training area, a studio, executive & general offices was designed to promote efficiency, teamwork and functionality.

For the design style of the space our client wanted a minimalist design, something cool, young and vibrant with clean look & a few colours(company colours) with white as the base.
