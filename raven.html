Project Raven

To design a modern and functional workspace that promotes efficiency and foster productivity as individual workers and as a team.

Year: 2021 Location: Lagos Client: Raven

Project Brief

Raven HQ is a 2 wing commercial office of about 400sqm. It is a hybrid design that maximixes the use of space creating private areas within an open plan office & introducing biophilic architecture to incorporate the team's love for outdoor living & wellness.

The design required a multi-faceted creative process that took into consideration the user goup, branding representation & interaction style. We went through a thorough thought process to ensure innovative design solutions were introduced to add value & improve the work flow. We believe that a good design should always bring greater value & elevate a space for optimum productivity of staff.
Designed with our successful formula: Work Rest Replenish & Collaborate

The office accomodates about 100 staff & consists of areas to work, Collaborate, eat, rest, socialize & have natural interaction. these spaces incluse a reception area, conference room, executive offices, training area, office pods, eating room, general office, a cafeteria & a loft.
