Project Baits

To design a modern and functional workspace that promotes efficiency and foster productivity as individual workers and as a team.

Year: 2021 Location: Lagos Client: Anon

Project Brief

This home office was designed for a tech bro alongside his significant other, a tech sis.

The project involves designing a home office that will serve as a comfortable and productive workspace for the client. The home office should reflect the client's personality and style while providing a functional and aesthetically pleasing environment.

The primary goal of the project is to create a space that is conducive to work and promotes productivity. The home office should have ample storage solutions, proper lighting, and ergonomic furniture. Additionally, the design should reflect the client's personality and style preferences.
