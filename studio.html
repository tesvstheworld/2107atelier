Gazebo

To design a modern and functional workspace that promotes efficiency and foster productivity as individual workers and as a team.

Year: 2021 Location: Lagos Client: AKB

Project Brief

The primary objective is to create a functional and aesthetically pleasing living space that meets the needs of a single individual.

The design goals are to create a multifunctional living space that serves as both a work and relaxation area, maximize the limited floor space by using innovative storage solutions, incorporate sustainable design elements, create a minimalist design that maximizes the feeling of spaciousness and calm, and adds personality & character to the space through the use of carefully selected materials, textures, and colors.
